How it works

Do I qualify?

Use the Net Credit Financial Service Loan Calculator to determine the right loan amount for your needs. Instantly view your potential repayments and complete our quick application form to receive a personalised pre-approval offer within minutes!

Can I afford it?

After receiving your pre-approval, submit your detailed income and expense information so we can assess your loan affordability. Once this assessment is complete, we will provide you with your final Net Credit Financial Service loan approval.

Let's finalise it!

Once your loan is approved, select your loan amount, provide your banking details, and upload the required documents to complete your application. Our simple and fast process ensures you have access to your loan within 48 hours.
